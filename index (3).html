About Us

Lighting the Path to Change with Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation

At Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation, we are more than just an organization – we are a movement, driven by the belief that education is the cornerstone of empowerment. Established under the Indian Societies Act of 1860, we stand as a beacon of hope in underserved communities, dedicated to breaking the chains of poverty through the transformative power of learning.

Join us in our quest to rewrite the narrative of education, one success story at a time. Together, let’s build a world where every child has the opportunity to dream, to learn, and to soar. Welcome to Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation – where hope is our currency, and education is our legacy.

Our Mission
At Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation, our mission is to provide quality education to children in slum areas by breaking barriers to access and opportunity. We aim to create a nurturing and inclusive learning environment where every child can thrive, grow, and realize their full potential. Through innovative teaching, community involvement, and strategic partnerships, we are committed to bridging the educational gap and fostering sustainable, long-term change.

Our Story
Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation began with a simple idea: to empower slum children through education. Inspired by her own experiences and driven by a passion for social justice, Sunita founded the organization under the Indian Societies Act of 1860. With the help of like-minded individuals, what started as a small initiative has grown into a powerful movement transforming countless lives. From humble beginnings to becoming a symbol of hope, our journey reflects resilience, compassion, and a shared belief that every child deserves a chance to learn and succeed.
